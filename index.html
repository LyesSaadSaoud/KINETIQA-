KINETIQA: Kinematic Integration Network with Enhanced Temporal Intelligence and Quality-driven Attention

For Precise Joint Angle Prediction in Exoskeleton-based Gait Analysis

Lyes Saad Saoud, Muhammad Irfan, Wadha Al Shehhi, Fatima Al Neyadi, Ahmed Al Harbi

Khalifa University, 2025 (Submitted to IEEE Access)

Code arXiv Paper PDF

Abstract

We present KINETIQA, a Transformer-based model for accurate and interpretable knee joint angle forecasting in exoskeleton-based gait analysis. The model integrates temporal convolutions and multi-head attention with quality-driven signal processing to capture biomechanical patterns under variable gait conditions. It achieves a 20% average reduction in Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE) compared to baselines like LSTM, GRU, TempoNet, and FocalGatedNet. Extensive evaluations demonstrate KINETIQA‚Äôs robustness to sensor noise (Gaussian, uniform, and salt-and-pepper), long-range prediction, and signal delay, making it suitable for wearable rehabilitation systems.
