Real-time exoskeleton control using robust knee angle forecasting.

Abstract

We introduce KINETIQA, a Transformer-based model tailored for real-time knee joint angle prediction in exoskeleton-assisted gait analysis. Leveraging recursive feature elimination, temporal convolutions, and quality-driven attention, KINETIQA delivers state-of-the-art accuracy while maintaining real-time inference capabilities. It surpasses models like Transformer, FocalGatedNet, TempoNet, and LSTM in long-horizon prediction and robustness to sensor noise (Gaussian, uniform, and salt-and-pepper). Experiments on clinical datasets demonstrate an average 20% MAPE reduction and high statistical significance across all noise types and time horizons.
