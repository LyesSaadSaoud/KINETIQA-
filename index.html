Real-time knee joint angle prediction in exoskeleton-assisted gait analysis.

Abstract

We present KINETIQA, a Transformer-based model tailored for real-time knee joint angle prediction in exoskeleton-assisted rehabilitation. It integrates temporal convolutions, recursive feature elimination, and quality-driven attention to enhance long-horizon gait forecasting while maintaining computational efficiency. KINETIQA significantly outperforms baselines like Transformer, TempoNet, LSTM, and FocalGatedNet, achieving over 20% MAPE reduction and demonstrating resilience to various sensor noise types including Gaussian, uniform, and salt-and-pepper noise.
